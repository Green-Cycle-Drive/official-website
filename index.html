Cleaning the world one event at a time

About

The Green Cycle Drive is an initiative which aims to raise awareness about climate change and to empower young environmentalists to make a difference in their community.

Our primary goals are to make everyone aware of proper waste disposal, to organize initiatives such as recycling drives for the protection of the environment, and to reduce the pollution we produce in our daily lives.

Achievements

Karama Recycling

We had hosted a recycling drive in which our members collected recyclable plastics, cardboards, newspapers, etc. from our community. The waste was taken to the Karama Recycling Centre on [Enter Date] where everyone who participated placed their garbage into the respective bins.

It was to our surprise that over 30 GCD members had arrived with huge amounts of waste, some of whom had brought over 50 kgs recyclable waste! It was extremely pleasing to discover that many of our members were so keen to protecting the world we live in.

In the end, more than 1000 kilograms of waste was collected and recycled with the help of our members. This event was one of the most successful recycling drives we have initiated.
