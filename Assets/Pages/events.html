  • Recycling Drive 2.0


    After the major success of last years recycling drive, we're hosting a second. Students will be required to collect at least 7kgs of recyclables such as cans, old newspapers, bottles, etc. from their home/neighborhood by 19th April. The collected waste will then be recycled at the Karama Recycling Center (Link for the location provided below) on 7th May. Certificates will be provided to all the participants.


    Location: Karama Recycling Center
    Date and Time: 7th May
    Eligible Grades: 6-12
