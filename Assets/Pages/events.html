  • Recycling Drive 2.0


    After the major success of last years recycling drive, we're hosting a second. The collected waste will then be recycled at the Karama Recycling Center (Link for the location provided below) on 7th May. Certificates will be provided to all the participants.


    Location: Karama Recycling Center
    Date and Time: 7th May
    Eligible Grades: 6-12
